The Problem

Your providers are eyeballing PDFs.
Your patients are paying for it.

Missed Recommendations

Hydration, sleep, key nutrients, peptides that should have been recommended. Every missed flag is revenue lost and a patient who doesn't feel better for another 3 months.

No Longitudinal View

Providers see one draw at a time. They can't see that ALT has been climbing for 4 draws straight or that hematocrit has been elevated for 17 months. Trends tell the story. Single snapshots don't.

Calls are tightening up. Providers don't have time to cross-reference 30 markers against symptoms, history, and protocol. Things slip through the cracks.

Patients who don't feel better leave. They cancel or go somewhere else looking for solutions. Every missed flag accelerates that timeline.
